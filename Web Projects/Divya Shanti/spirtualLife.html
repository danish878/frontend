Spirtual Life

We have three Community Churches and several worship centers. Apart from the regular services on Sundays, we have Sunday schools for children in five areas (New Lingarajapuram, Lingarajapuram, Baglur, Venkateshpuram and K.K.Halli) and on the whole we have about 400 children who attend the Sunday schools.

For the young people regular youth meetings are conducted. They have a youth choir and a counseling center. The women in the community meet once a week for a fellowship, where they learn more about Jesus and pray together. People have been greatly blessed by the early morning prayer which has been started recently.

The pastors lead in a time of praise and worship. Many testimonies have started pouring in from people who have experienced God's love and have confidence to face each day with a renewed spirit. We meet every day for prayer from 5:30 am. to 6:30 am which has greatly strengthened our witness.
