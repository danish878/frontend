Education

Divya Shanti High School


Divya Shanthi education programmes focus on children from the lowest income band. Total family income is under Rs 3000/- (USD 75 & UKÂ£ 40) per month. English medium schooling is the road to success and exit from poverty.

Average monthly fee in a good English medium school which by definition is a private institution and not a Government run school is about Rs 800 per child. This is beyond the means of poor families. Poor families are prepared for any sacrifice to get their children an English medium education.



VOCATIONAL TRAINING

Divya Shanthi Trust is engaged in vocational training for Youth from the poorest backgrounds in South India since 1975. It has set up vocational training programme for young women in tailoring, embroidery and garment making, for the young men in tool making, machining, metal fabrication and fibre glass moulding trades, for young men and women in motorcycle and car repairs and maintenance.

Some Vocational training programme like the tailoring, motorised vehicle repairs followed state reocgnised syllabi and the students received state recognised certificates. Other training programmes received certificates given by the state. The trainees were also part of the production centre and worked on producing goods for the market.
